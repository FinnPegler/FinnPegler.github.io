Hi Founders And Coders!

My name is Finn and thanks for visiting my website for the Founders & Coders spring 2020 application.

A little about me: as my day job, I own and remotely run a US based cleaning company. After five solitary years bootstrapping and running the company I have decided it is time for a new challenge. I have replaced myself in day-to-day operations and left to concentrate on learning to code.

My plan is: to find a position as a junior front-end developer in the next 6 months. Being accepted to Founders And Coders would help a great deal in this pursuit and mean the world to me.

Why FAC might want me: I love teaching people. While studying at University I would set up study groups where each member would take a chapter and teach the group. I enjoy synthesising new knowledge to a level that I can explain it in (hopefully) simple terms. I have found it is the best way to learn for me, personally.
